PCOs & PCOD

PCOS and PCOD are ovarian disorders causing hormonal imbalance. PCOS is more severe, leading to irregular periods, high androgens, and metabolic issues, while PCOD is milder. Both can be managed with lifestyle changes and medication.

Sexually Transmitted Infections

STIs are infections spread through sexual contact. Common STIs include HIV, chlamydia, gonorrhea, syphilis etc. Symptoms vary but may include sores, discharge, pain, or no symptoms at all. Prevention includes safe sex practices, regular testing, and vaccination. Early diagnosis and treatment help prevent complications.

Infertility

*Infertility* is the inability to conceive after a year of unprotected sex, affecting both men and women. Causes include hormonal imbalances, reproductive disorders, age, and lifestyle factors. Treatment options include medication, IVF, and lifestyle changes.
